The First Post: La Reunion

1 January 1970

I am extremely happy to present the first postcard I've received. It is from Léa, who lives on a French island la Réunion.

As Léa wrote in her postcard: Koifé? is creole for what's up?, and creole dialect is widely spoken on Réunion (even though it is a French island).

In one of the pictures we can also see some cold lava, which according to Léa proves that the island tries to kill its inhabitants every once in a while :)

Hello, my name is Anna

1 January 1970

I'm fascinated by islands and if it only was possible, i would visit all of them. But for now, I would like to try to reach as many of them as possible in a bit different way: my big dream is to receive a postcard (or anything else that can be sent) from every island that has a postbox!

I would be extremely happy if I could get something from your island. It may be a postcard or an envelope with something inside, like e.g. a leaf that you found, or a piece of wrap from your favourite candy, or a simple drawing that you made. Anything!

You can also write something about yourself and your island, what do you do? how is the weather? what is the view from your window?

Looking forward to your mail! (I would be also glad to send you something back, from where I am right now) And of course everything that you send will be kept here on this website, so that everyone can see how wonderful all those islands are!

Maybe one day, with your help, I will be able to get a small piece of every island there is.
